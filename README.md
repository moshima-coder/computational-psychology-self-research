# Computational Self Research

This repository contains my research notes and project drafts on computational approaches to the self, self-discrepancy, self-concept clarity, core beliefs, defense mechanisms, and coping.

## Research Theme

My current research interest is to understand how repeated adaptation to self-discrepancy may contribute to the formation of core beliefs and psychological distress.

## Main Questions

1. How do discrepancies between self-representations affect self-concept clarity?
2. How are self-discrepancies related to core beliefs?
3. Do defense mechanisms and coping strategies mediate the relationship between self-discrepancy and psychological distress?
4. Can computational approaches help model the formation of maladaptive self-beliefs?
